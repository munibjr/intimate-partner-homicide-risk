# Ethical Framework

## What This System Does

✅ Identifies escalation patterns
✅ Scores relative risk (0-100)
✅ Explains which factors drive risk
✅ Recommends protective actions

## What This System Does NOT Do

❌ Predict that X will kill Y
❌ Automatically trigger actions
❌ Make guilt/innocence judgments
❌ Replace professional judgment

## Safeguards

### Decision Support, Not Automation
- Output: "HIGH escalation risk"
- NOT: "Arrest this person"
- Professional reviews and decides

### Explainability Required
- Every score shows WHY
- Top factors clearly stated
- Police can verify reasoning

### Bias Auditing
- Check for systematic bias by demographics
- False positive analysis
- Regular audits for drift

## Known Limitations

1. Synthetic data (not real)
2. Norwegian-specific patterns
3. All ML has bias potential
4. Requires human oversight

## Recommended Use

✅ Case prioritization
✅ Victim safety planning
✅ Multi-agency coordination
✅ Training professionals

## Not Recommended

❌ Automatic action triggers
❌ Sole basis for decisions
❌ Public surveillance
